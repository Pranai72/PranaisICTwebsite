Chapter 3.1

This chapter covers Many forms of secondary storage and computers and compares the

advantages and disadvantages of each type. In chapter 1 you will learnt about the

primary memory, known as RAM and ROM. We will consider a number of

storage devices later. First it is important to consider why we need to backup data

using these devices and also how data is accessed.
